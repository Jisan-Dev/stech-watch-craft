To ensure a smooth and personalized experience, Face Craft may collect and use certain information, such as:

  • Device model and operating system version

  • Basic account identifiers (for syncing or backup features)

All information collected is used solely to personalize your device, ad improve overall performance. All information collected is handled securely and only used within this scope. Face Craft is designed for Apple Watch users to explore creative watch faces, collections, and sync them to their devices with ease.

We may use limited, aggregated, non-personally identifying information that could gather data to help identify performance issues and user trends. These may include, but are not limited to:

  • Firebase Analytics (for usage insights and crash analytics)

2. Log Information

When you use the Face Craft app, certain technical information is automatically collected in the event of an error or crash. This log information can include details like:

This is used solely for debugging and improving app stability. It is not linked to any personally identifying information.
